Once upon a time, there was a beautiful forest where many animals lived together.

Page 1

Among those animals, there lived a hare and a tortoise. The hare was known for being loud and lazy.

The tortoise was known for being quiet and hardworking.

Page 2

One day, hare was having a conversation with the tortoise and began bragging about himself. He told the tortoise, I am the fastest animal in town. The hare did not stop there, he went on to insult the tortoise about being slow.

Page 3

The tortoise felt bad. He thought, you may be the fastest animal, but I am hard working and won't give up.

Page 4

He wanted to prove the hare wrong and challenged him to race with him. Hare readily accepted the challenge thinking he would further insult the tortoise.

Page 5

The race began the next week. All animals gathered to see who wins the race.

The hare raced sway sprinting as fast as he could. The tortoise began slowly and steadily.

Page 6

After running at full speed for several minutes, the hare got tired. He looked down the road but could not find the tortoise anywhere in sight.

Page 7

He thought The tortoise is slow, and I am fast. I could relax for a bit and afford to win the race. He slept peacefully on the grass.

Page 8

While the hare napped, the tortoise kept walking. He overtook the hare and almost reached the finish line.

Page 9

Hare woke up and started running as fast as he could. But tortoise crossed the finish line first and won the race amidst the cheering of all other animals.

Moral of the story: Slow and steady wins the race

Page 10

Click on name of the animal that won the race?
